The Gaudí Mystery: Part 1

Level: B1 | Focus: Will vs Going To & Asking Directions

1. Match the Words (Pre-reading)

Click an English word, then click its Spanish translation.

💡 Tip: Double-click or select any word you don't know to translate and save it to My Vocabulary!

It was a cold evening in Barcelona. Pau, a telecommunications student, was walking in the narrow streets of the Raval neighborhood. He had created an advanced Artificial Intelligence code to solve historical mysteries. "I am going to test this AI code tonight," he thought. He decided to walk to the city center. He went straight down the street, crossed the square, and turned left at the corner.

Meanwhile, Sofia, a psychology student, was standing near the old buildings of the Universal Exhibition in Montjuïc. She had studied the behavior of historical geniuses for many years. Suddenly, she received a strange message on her phone. It was an anonymous map of Barcelona. "This is very weird, but I will follow the map," she decided spontaneously.

Sofia needed to find the next location, so she asked a police officer for directions. "Excuse me, how do I get to the Spotify Camp Nou stadium?" she asked. The officer pointed down the road. "Go straight for two blocks, then turn right at the traffic light, and take the subway," he explained.

At the same time, Nico, a professional boxer, was training at the Carmel Bunkers. He had found a strange metallic artifact while running near the beach in Barceloneta earlier that morning. The artifact was vibrating in his pocket. "I am going to take this to a technology expert tomorrow," Nico promised himself.

The three young people did not know each other. They were completely different, but their paths were going to cross. Sofia followed the officer's directions and arrived at the subway station near the Spotify Camp Nou, which was currently under construction. The massive stadium looked more impressive in the dark.

Pau arrived at the same station. His laptop was beeping loudly. "If my AI is correct, the source of the strange signal is exactly here," he muttered. Just then, Nico walked out of the subway. The metallic artifact in his hand was glowing bright blue.

Pau looked at Nico's artifact, and Sofia looked at Pau's computer screen. "Your artifact is connected to my AI code!" Pau said, surprised. Sofia smiled. "And my anonymous map brought me exactly to you two."

They realized they were all looking for the same ancient mystery related to Antoni Gaudí, hidden beneath the modern city. "What are we going to do now?" Nico asked, looking at the glowing metal. "We are going to find the truth together," Sofia replied firmly. Pau nodded. "I will hack the next security level of the map right now," he declared.

A strong friendship had begun. They knew the journey was going to be dangerous, but they were ready to uncover the greatest secret of Barcelona.

2. Comprehension Quiz (Cuestionario)

1. How did Sofia ask for directions to the stadium?

2. What did the police officer tell Sofia to do?

3. Where did Nico find the strange metallic artifact?

4. What did Pau decide to do at the end of the story?
