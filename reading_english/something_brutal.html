Something Brutal Happened Today

Level: B1 | Focus: First Conditional (If + Present, Will + Infinitive)

1. Match the Words (Pre-reading)

Click an English word, then click its Spanish translation.

💡 Tip: Pay attention to the If sentences. Double-click any word you don't know to translate and save it!

Joan is a nineteen-year-old boy. He moved from Venezuela to Spain last year. He wants to study psychology at the university, and he absolutely loves reading comics. He is a normal teenager who worries about his exams, his future, and paying the rent. But today, something brutal happened to him.

Joan was walking home from the library. He was carrying a heavy box of psychology books. Suddenly, a car lost control and drove onto the sidewalk at high speed. Joan did not have time to jump. He simply put his hands out to protect himself.

The car hit his hands and stopped immediately. The metal of the car was completely destroyed, but Joan did not have a single scratch on his body. The driver was shocked, but safe. Joan panicked and ran all the way home, faster than he had ever run before.

He locked his door and looked at his hands in the mirror. "This is impossible," he thought. "I am a normal student. If I tell my friends about this, they will think I am completely crazy."

"Why the hell is this happening to me?" he whispered, holding his head. He tried to find a logical explanation. Suddenly, he remembered a strange event from his childhood in Venezuela. When he was eight years old, he was playing near a river in the mountains of Mérida. He found a strange, glowing rock in the water. When he touched it, he fell into a coma with a high fever for three days. The doctors could not explain his miraculous recovery. "If that rock changed my DNA, my body will continue to mutate," he realized with fear.

He sat on his bed and tried to use his psychology knowledge to stay calm. "If I have super strength, my life will change forever," he reasoned. "If the government discovers me, they will take me to a secret laboratory for experiments."

He decided to test his new abilities in secret. He tried to lift his heavy wooden bed with just one hand. It went up easily, as light as a feather! "If I can do this, I will be able to help many people," he whispered. "But if I wear a colorful cape like in the comics, I will look ridiculous."

Joan decided to be practical and subtle. He opened his closet and put on a simple dark hoodie. "If I hide my face with this hood, nobody will know my secret identity."

He knew the real world was not a comic book. There were no magical supervillains in his city, just normal people who sometimes needed a silent hero. "If I am careful, I will survive this," he promised himself. He put on his hoodie and looked at the streets below. His ordinary life was over, but a new, extraordinary chapter had just begun.

2. Comprehension Quiz (Cuestionario)

1. Where is Joan originally from?

2. What happens if Joan tells his friends about the car accident?

3. Why doesn't Joan want the government to discover him?

4. What does Joan decide to wear to hide his identity?
