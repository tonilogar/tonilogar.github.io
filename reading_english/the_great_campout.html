The Great Campout

Level: A2 (Elementary)

1. Match the Words (Pre-reading)

Click an English word, then click its Spanish translation.

Last weekend, Sarah and her friends decided to go camping. They packed their bags, cooked some food, and traveled to the mountains. The weather looked beautiful when they arrived. They walked for an hour and reached a clear lake.

First, they pitched their tents near the water. Then, they collected wood for a fire. As the sun dropped, the air turned cold. Sarah started the fire, and her friends roasted marshmallows. They talked, laughed, and listened to the sounds of the forest.

Suddenly, they noticed a strange noise behind a tree. They stopped talking and looked. It sounded like footsteps. Someone stepped on a branch. They waited in silence. A small deer appeared. It looked at them for a second, turned around, and hopped away. They all breathed a sigh of relief and smiled.

They finished their snacks, cleaned the area, and crawled into their sleeping bags. The next morning, they packed everything and returned home. They enjoyed the trip very much and promised to return next summer.

Comprehension Quiz (Cuestionario)

1. Where did Sarah and her friends pitch their tents?

2. What did they do after they collected wood?

3. What made the strange noise behind the tree?

4. Which tense is mostly used in this story?
